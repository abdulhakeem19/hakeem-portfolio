# 12 · Version Roadmap — pillars, not feature dumps

> **Plan versions around strengthening the core promise, never around random features.**
>
> **Know what you can spend today → Make better decisions → Reach financial freedom faster.**

Every version deepens **one of three pillars** — anything that doesn't is demoted or dropped:

1. **Know** — Reality Balance / Safe-to-Spend (clarity)
2. **Decide** — Can I Buy This? (decision intelligence)
3. **Escape** — Freedom & debt reduction (future outcomes)

The 2-year bet — Affora's category-defining identity:
> **"Affora shows how today's spending decisions affect your future freedom."**
> (Bigger than budgeting / expense tracking / finance management.)

**Status legend:** ✅ done · 🟡 partial · ⬜ pending · 🔮 future bet (don't build yet)

> **Reconciliation note:** the V1 *refocus* front-loaded most of the "launch track"
> (V1.1–V1.3) and part of V1.4 **before** the first store upload. So the **initial Play
> Store release ships as V1.0 but already contains those items.** They're marked ✅ here and
> listed under "What ships in the initial release" below.

## V1.0 — **Clarity** (the initial Play Store release)
> Know exactly what you can spend today.

Core, all ✅: Reality Balance · Safe-to-Spend · Auto-Capture · Goals · Debt · Bills ·
Can I Buy This · Couples · Forecast · Net Worth · Reports · Gamification.
Plus the launch-track items below (front-loaded).

**Validate after launch:** daily active usage · retention · whether users *understand*
Safe-to-Spend.

## V1.1 — **Focus** ✅ (shipped in the initial release)
- ✅ Home declutter — removed Coach card, Last-Month card, Streak row, Quick-Actions.
- ✅ Navigation re-tier — Reports/Net Worth/Forecast/Accounts/Categories moved to secondary
  (Profile/Progress); Home is a decision surface.
- ✅ Play Billing — `affora_pro_monthly` subscription, purchase + restore + entitlement.
- **Metric:** can a new user understand Affora in <10 seconds?

## V1.2 — **Freedom** 🟡 (mostly shipped)
- ✅ **Turning Points** — "Upcoming Freedom" Home card (next debt/EMI payoff, ₹ freed,
  Safe-to-Spend uplift).
- 🟡 "Next Major Milestone" — the **Goal Impact** card ships ✅; a dedicated milestone view is
  light (see Freedom Timeline, V2.0).
- **Metric:** users emotionally connect with future outcomes.

## V1.3 — **Decision Intelligence** 🟡 (core shipped)
- ✅ **Can I Buy This 2.0 / Purchase Consequences** — leads with Safe-to-Spend before→after,
  goal delay, debt impact, verdict (score demoted to support).
- ⬜ **Alternatives** — "Wait 7 days → no goal impact / Wait until salary → zero impact."
- **Metric:** users consult Affora *before* purchases.

## V1.4 — **Behavior Change** 🟡 (started)
- ✅ **Safe-to-Spend adherence** — "within target N of M days" on Progress.
- ⬜ **Monthly Review** — "you saved ₹4,800 more than last month" (the old last-month recap
  was removed from Home; a proper review screen is pending).
- ⬜ **Freedom Score** — real progress (debt ratio + savings + goal progress), not gamified
  fluff.

## V1.5 — **Relationships** ⬜
- ⬜ Couples 2.0 — shared debt goals, shared emergency funds, financial milestones.
- ⬜ Joint Freedom Timeline — "Debt free: Mar 2027 · House deposit: Dec 2028."
- **Metric:** couples use Affora together weekly.

---

## V2.0 — **Financial Operating System** 🔮 (category-defining)
- ⬜ **Freedom Timeline** — a visual roadmap (card cleared → EMI ends → emergency fund →
  first ₹1 lakh → debt free). Likely Affora's signature feature.
- ⬜ **Scenario Planning** — "pay ₹5,000 extra/mo → debt-free 4 months sooner";
  "stop Zomato → +₹72/day Safe-to-Spend."
- ⬜ **Life Events** — new salary / marriage / new loan → show consequences immediately.

## V2.5 — **Prediction** 🔮
- ⬜ Smart forecasting — predict overspending / debt risk / goal delays *before* they happen.
  (Real prediction, not AI chat.)

## V3.0 — **Financial Coach** 🔮 (AI only here, not before)
- ⬜ AI Coach that reasons over Affora's real data — "How can I free ₹5,000/month?",
  "Why is Safe-to-Spend falling?". Build only after PMF; don't fix positioning with AI.

---

## What ships in the initial Play Store release (V1.0)
The full core **plus** the front-loaded launch track:
✅ Home decluttered to a decision surface · ✅ navigation re-tiered · ✅ Play Billing ·
✅ Turning Points · ✅ Purchase Consequences · ✅ Safe-to-Spend adherence · ✅ offline-first
sync + reinstall restore · ✅ couples mode.

**This is a solid release. Focus = ship it,** then validate the V1.0 metrics before building more.

## Pending (post-launch, in priority order)
1. ⬜ Purchase **Alternatives** (V1.3 finish) — cheap, high-signal.
2. ⬜ **Monthly Review** + **Freedom Score** (V1.4).
3. ⬜ **Couples 2.0** + Joint Freedom Timeline (V1.5).
4. 🔮 **Freedom Timeline** + **Scenario Planning** (V2.0 — the category-defining bet).
5. 🔮 Prediction (V2.5) → AI Coach (V3.0), in that order.

## Never build (pulls Affora toward a generic finance app)
❌ Stock / crypto / mutual-fund tracking · ❌ loan / credit-card / insurance marketplaces ·
❌ cashback / rewards · ❌ expense-category obsession · ❌ complex accounting.
